we've all heard about the worlds 7 wonders, but how much do we know about them?

The seven ancient wonders of the world were magnificient man-made landmarks from ancient civilizations. The firsst known of these wonders dates back to 2nd and 1st BC.
This site will explore the 7 wonders and their location, history, cultural significance, and travlel information for those interested in visiting.







It's important to remember that except for the Great Pyramid of Giza, the other wonders no longer exist in their original form. However, visiting their locations still offers a rich historical and cultural experience as you explore the remnants and related archaeological sites.
