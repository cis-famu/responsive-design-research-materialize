Colossus of Rhodes

This Wonder is a depiction of the sun god Helios and was located in Rhodes, Greece. Made of bronze and iron, The Colossus of Rhodes stood at 105 feet. The 12 years of work it took to make the statues were destroyed during an earthquake in either 225 or 226 BCE. The statue was then broken into pieces for scraps when Rhodes was raided in 654 CE.
