Lighthouse of Alexandria

This Wonder was built by Sostratus of Cnidus and was located on the island of Pharos in Alexandria, Egypt. It is believed that either a statue of Alexander the Great or Ptolemy I Soter as Helios was next to the lighthouse. The beacon was replaced with a small mosque in the Middle Ages. The Lighthouse of Alexandria was truly a technological feat.
