Statue of Zeus at Olympia

This Wonder was sculpted by Phidias. The statue of Zeus would have been found in the Temple of Zeus in Olympia. In his right hand is a statue of Nike, and in his left is a scepter with an eagle perched on it. The temple which housed the statue was destroyed in 426 CE.
