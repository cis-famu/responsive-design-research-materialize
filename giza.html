The Great pyramids of Giza

Location: Giza, Egypt

Historical Notes:
- Time Period: Built around 2560 BCE during the reign of Pharaoh Khufu.

- Significance: The only wonder of the ancient world that still exists today.

- Construction: Comprised of approximately 2.3 million limestone blocks.

- Purpose: Served as a tomb for Pharaoh Khufu.

- Additional Information: The Great Pyramid was the tallest man-made structure in the world for over 3,800 years.



Best time to visit

- Optimal Time: October to April

- Reason: Mild temperatures compared to the scorching summer months. Also, a period of fewer sandstorms.

- Note: December and January are peak tourist seasons due to the holiday period, hence expect larger crowds.









General Travel Tips:

- Early Planning: Ensure to plan your visit well in advance to manage accommodations and itineraries better.

- Local Events: Check for any local events or festivals that might enrich your visit.

- Safety: Always consider the current political and social situation of the destination, ensuring it's safe for tourists.

- Pandemic Guidelines: Be sure to check any travel restrictions or guidelines due to ongoing global health situations.
