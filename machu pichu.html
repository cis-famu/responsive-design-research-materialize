Temple of Artemis

The Temple of Artemis is yet another one of the 7 wonders of the world. This wonder would have been located in what is now Turkey. Croesus, the king of Lydia, built the temple in around 550 BCE. It would later be rebuilt in 356 BCE after it was burned down. It was later destroyed in the Goth invasion in 262 CE. However, copies of the statue of Artemis survived
