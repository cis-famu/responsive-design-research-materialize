Hanging Garden of Babylon

It is speculated that this Wonder was located near the royal palace in Babylon, now modern-day southern Iraq. This wonder has been the protagonist of many classical writers as they describe its beauty. Research suggests that the Hanging Gardens were “designed to imitate a natural mountain landscape and were watered by a novel system of irrigation.” (Britannica)
