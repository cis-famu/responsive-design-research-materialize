Mausoleum at Halicarnassus

This Wonder is located in Halicarnassus, Turkey, and is the tomb of Mausolus, who was the ruler of Caria. The Mausoleum was designed by Pythius and Satyros. The sides of the Mausoleum are intricately decorated with the Greek artists Scopas, Bryaxis, Leochares, and Timotheus. It is speculated that an earthquake destroyed the Mausoleum sometime between the 11th and the 15th century CE. However, one may see fragments of the Mausoleum being preserved in the British Museum.
